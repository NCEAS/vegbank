Help for 'Match your plants to VegBank plants'

This form is used to match your plant names (found in several of your loading tables) to vegbank plant concepts. This process will take a modest amount of time is you have a lot of plant names. Names that are not matches WILL BE ADDED to the vegbank module, but there information must be included in your species list in order for this to happen. @vegbank_footer_html_tworow@

VegBranch Help:

Build USDA list for NPS-PLOTS data entry

Screenshot (click to enlarge in new window):

This form creates a list of USDA plants that are used in entering plants in the data entry process. THIS IS A REQUIRED STEP, though you will only have to do it once (unless you switch to a different loading module).

Press the red highlighted button: Build USDA PLANTS list, then close the form. To see the contents of the list, please see help on the form: Enter Species for a plot: NPS-PLOTS entry forms in VegBranch.

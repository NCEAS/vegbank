The ESA Standards Document:

Guidelines for Describing Associations and Alliances of the U.S. National Vegetation Classification

NEW! Version 3.0 (November 2003)

This is the first version in which all parts (sections, glossary, literature cited, appendices, tables, figures, and text boxes) are complete. It contains the new definitions for association and alliance. Although the document will be improved upon in the future, no part of it is still pending. This concludes an effort first talked about when the Panel met at the June 1995 IAVS conference in Houston, over eight years ago.

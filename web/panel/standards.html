Version 1.0 of the ESA Standards released

The ESA Vegetation Panel has completed Version 1.0 of the Standards for Associations and Alliances of the US National Vegetation Classification. The document is available as a pdf file [1.5M] and as a zipped doc file [138K].

The Panel welcomes proposals for revisions. Proposals should be directed to the Chair of the Panel.

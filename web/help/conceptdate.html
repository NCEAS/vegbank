VegBank Concept Date

VegBank's plant concept and community concepts allow Party Perspectives to change over time. You can specify a date on the query page to find out what a party's view of a plant or community was as of that date. If you do not specify a date on the query, today's date is used and current Party Perspectives will be returned to you.

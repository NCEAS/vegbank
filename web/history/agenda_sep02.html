General goals

1. We need to review the progress to date and figure out what to do next to refine our products through the end of the first funding cycle, which ends in December. Our primary employee on the project, John Harris, plans to be rotating off very soon (gone half of Sept, all of Oct, and then for good at the beginning of December). Our goal is to launch a prototype of VegBank very soon (late September?), so as to start accumulating user feedback and public comment. We especially need to assess what additional work is critical for that launch, and which additions can wait for a subsequent upgrade.

Please review the current status of the product. There will be a lot of evolution this week, so perhaps best to look on or after September 1.

http://www.vegbank.org/

2. We need to assess progress to date and future needs regards our desk-top client tool VegBranch.

http://vegbank.nceas.ucsb.edu/vegbranch/vegbranch.html

3. We plan to discuss with Wiser, Cooper, Boyle, Tart and Hennenkens how best to interface our varied efforts and assure maximal compatibility.

Cooperators and new members need to read the supporting data model documents.

For information on VegBank and the plots data model:

http://vegbank.nceas.ucsb.edu/vegbank/pubs/NBII_Jan02_VegBank_files/frame.htm (IE)

http://vegbank.nceas.ucsb.edu/vegbank/design/plots.html

For information on the taxonomic datamodel:

http://vegbank.nceas.ucsb.edu/vegbank/pubs/NBII_Jan02_Taxa_files/frame.htm (IE)

http://vegbank.nceas.ucsb.edu/vegbank/design/plants.html

Also, it might help to read the grant proposal for the next phase of the work.

http://www.bio.unc.edu/faculty/peet/lab/Proposals/NSF_VegBank_2002.pdf

4. Long-range planning. We should discuss long-range planning and how to proceed with execution of phase two. For this it would help if all were to read the proposal linked above.

My VegBank Account

Register to become a VegBank User.

  • If you don't yet have an account and would like to create one, please follow the above link. Please note that you do not need to register to access data in VegBank, but you will have to register and request certification to add data. You may also want to register to permanently keep datasets (certification not required).

Login or Logout

  • Logging in allows you to access your account and personal settings, such as datasets. Logging out after you are finished protects your account from being changed by the next user (on your computer). Note that VegBank will prompt you to log in if you try to access a feature that requires logging in.

Edit Personal information

  • View or change your registration information

Request Certification

  • Request certification to perform advanced functions in VegBank, such as loading plots or annotating data

Datasets

  • Here, you can manage and query using sets of data, including sets of plots, plants, and communities.

View/Manage Your Uploaded Plots

  • If you have contributed plots to VegBank, you may view and manage them here. You'll be able to see the embargo status of each plot, and soon, you'll be able to add, renew, or cancel embargoes for your plots.

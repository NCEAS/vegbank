VegBank User Certification

The ESA Vegetation Panel will provide a certification program for VegBank users. Certification is needed for at least two reasons.

1. The quality a user attaches to a plot in the archive is likely to vary with his or her perception of the workers who collected and submitted the data. In addition, users need a way to evaluate the credentials of an individual who has contributed an annotation (note) or interpretation (identification of a plot observation to community type, or a plant observation to species/taxon). One function of certification is to provide the user with a means of evaluating the ability of an unknown worker.

2. The quality of a plot in the archive will increase as additional information is added and interpretation are made. Not all users are qualified to add information to plot records, nor will all users understand the database and it users well enough to know how best to add value to plot records. Certain types of annotation or interpretation will require user certification.

We currently recognize 4 levels of VegBank users.

Registered User. This status requires self registration. Registration is automatic and requires only continued good behavior.

Professional User. Professional users are expected to demonstrate familiarity with (1) vegetation sampling methodology, (2) plant identification, (3) the US National Vegetation Classification, and (4) the capabilities and applications of the VegBank archive.

Senior User. This status requires at least one year of experience as a Profession User (or equivalent experience), and demonstrated proficiency with at least two of the four areas of experience required of Professional Users.

Expert User. The ESA Vegetation Panel Certification Subcommittee will occasional honor especially well qualified and experienced users with the rank of Expert User.

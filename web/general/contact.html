VegBank Users Community Lists

These lists are public and anyone may join them. Messages can be posted with questions about vegbank, and users can post responses to these questions. Click "join" to subscribe to a list, and "read" to browse the archive, as all emails to the lists below will be archived publicly. If you'd like to post a question to a list, please join the list first. You'll receive an email with instructions about how to post a message and change your options, such as reducing the amount of email you receive from these lists. You can elect to receive daily digests, or no email at all (in which case you can still browse the list online, and post messages to it).

users@vegbank.org - join | read - This list is for questions and issues about vegbank.

vegbranch@vegbank.org - join | read - Subscribe to this list to discuss issues and help for VegBranch, the client database for VegBank.

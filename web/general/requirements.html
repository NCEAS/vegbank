VegBank Browser and Computer Requirements

This page describes the minimum requirements your computer and web browser must meet in order for VegBank to function properly on your computer. A list of specific known bugs in VegBank can be found here.

Browser Requirements and Recommendations

  • An up-to-date browser, such as:
    • Mozilla Firefox 1.0 recommended - fully functional
    • Internet Explorer 6.0 Win | Mac - fully functional
    • Netscape 7.0 + - fully functional
    • Safari 2.0 - fully functional
    • Opera 8.0 - some advanced features, such as datacart, do not work.
  • JavaScript enabled.
    • Specifically, Javascript is necessary for queries and datacart functions. Javascript is also used for highlighting in the tutorial and FAQ.
    • If you are having problems with datacart or queries, check your browser properties to see if Javascript is enabled. Also, check any third party browser security programs like Norton Internet Security, to see if they are restricting Javascript.
  • Frames enabled.
    • Frames are used in the tutorial and in some VegBranch documentation. Limited functionality without frames may or may not exist.
  • Cookies enabled.
    • Cookies are used if you login to remember who you are. Additionally, the datacart uses a session cookie to remember who you are if you add plots to the datacart without logging in.

Computer Requirements and Recommendations

  • An up-to-date web browser, as described above.
  • A mouse.
  • 256MB RAM is recommended for larger downloads and page displays.
  • VegBranch requirements and recommendations:
    • REQUIRED:
      • Microsoft Access, version 2000 or higher (2002 or higher is recommended)
      • A Program to unzip the downloaded files, such as WinZip, PKZip, or Windows XP (which includes an unzipping utility).
      • Enough free hard drive space for VegBranch and the VegBank Module, usually at least 750MB.
    • Recommended: (These a general estimates, slight reductions from these will likely still work.)
      • At least 256MB RAM.
      • At least 1.5 GB free hard drive space (to allow extra space for backup databases).
      • At least 1.0 GHz processor.
